Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish featuring a baked dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings such as vegetables, meats, or herbs.

This pizza recipe creates a crispy, golden crust with melty cheese and flavorful toppings. Perfect for a family dinner or a weekend treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone and spread tomato sauce evenly over the surface.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add toppings such as pepperoni, bell peppers, olives, and season with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  7. Remove from oven, add fresh basil leaves if desired, slice, and serve hot.
