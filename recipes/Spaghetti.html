Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish served with a rich tomato sauce, seasoned with garlic, herbs, and optionally meatballs or vegetables. It's a quick and satisfying meal for lunch or dinner.

This spaghetti recipe is simple yet flavorful, perfect for family dinners or a cozy meal at home. Pair it with Parmesan cheese for extra taste.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey (optional)
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add ground meat (if using) and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce and oregano to the skillet. Simmer for 10-15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss the cooked spaghetti noodles with the sauce until fully coated.
  6. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan and fresh basil if desired.
