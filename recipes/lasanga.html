Lasanga

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It is hearty, comforting, and perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

This lasagna recipe combines a flavorful tomato-based meat sauce with layers of pasta and cheese for a deliciously baked dish. It’s sure to satisfy both kids and adults alike!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, cook the ground beef with garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce and dried basil to the meat. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with the egg, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer 3 noodles, half of the ricotta mixture, some mozzarella, and meat sauce. Repeat for another layer.
  8. Top with the remaining noodles, meat sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until cheese is golden.
  10. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
